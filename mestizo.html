Mestizo

Mestizo brings to London the quintessential Mexican culture.
This restaurant-bar is a must-go for those eager to experience the authentic Mexican cuisine.
From quesadillas to enchilladas, the rich menu will leave you spoilt of choice.
Dont't forget to drop by the cocktail bar.
Counting more than 200 tequilas, it has one of the largest collections in London. Margaritas are not to be missed.
